## 💡 Technical Details

### Tailwind Size Classes:
- `h-16 w-16` = 64px (4rem)
- `h-14 w-14` = 56px (3.5rem)
- `h-24 w-24` = 96px (6rem)

### Padding Classes:
- `p-1.5` = 6px (0.375rem)
- `p-2` = 8px (0.5rem)
